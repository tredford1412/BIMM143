The boxplot illustrates the expression levels of ORMDL3 across different genotypes (A/A, A/G, and G/G). From the plot, we can observe that individuals with the A/A genotype tend to exhibit higher relative expression levels compared to those with the G/G genotype. Meanwhile, the A/G genotype displays an intermediate distribution, with the highest median expression among the three groups. This suggests that the SNP has an impact on ORMDL3 expression, where the presence of the G allele is associated with a lower expression level. Given these findings, it is likely that this SNP plays a role in regulating ORMDL3 expression, which may be relevant for further genetic and functional studies.
